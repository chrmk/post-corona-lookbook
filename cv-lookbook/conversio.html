Conversio

Game Concept

Conversio is both a fun and mindful game for anyone who wants to have a meaningful conversations with friends, family and partners. It fosters an exchange that goes beyond the superficial “how are you doing” and enables you to get to know each other even better. It can be played with family, friends, work colleagues - anyone you want to deepen your connection even more. The core of the game is an intelligent algorithm fitting your goals for the conversation with those of your co-players, by choosing in an optimized way out of large pool of questions.

Versatility

The questions vary depending on the conversation goals and setting. It can be played with family, friends, and colleagues.

Intimacy

The game creates a positive environment to share feelings and experiences. It promotes communication, honesty, and respect.

Fun

Conversio playfully assists you in a journey of self-knowledge and exchange. It is one of the few games where everybody wins!

Start of the Game

Every player defines the desired areas of interest for oneself and for the other players. Conversio's algorithm defines the pool of questions that will be answered during the game.

Reflection-Square

If you are on a reflection-square during your turn, you answer the given question with the medium determined on the card. Take your time, and be honest! After answering, move the speificied number of squares determined on the question card.

Discover-Square

If you are on a discover-square, the others have to answer a question about you with the medium determined on the card. Answer the question as well, and make sure to hide it. Every player who answered correctly moves the specified number of squares on the card.

End of the Game

The game ends when the first player reaches the end of the board. However, that does not mean that he won, it just signals the end of the game. In Conversio, everybody wins!
